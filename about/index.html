to make one’s way with a slow heavy pace against resistance

As I’ve grown older, my rate of learning new things has dropped drastically. I’ll use this site as a notebook where I systematically try to learn new things, keep up with some literature on areas tangentially related to my work. Sometimes, I’ll also ramble about things of fleeting interest, for the sake of my sanity. The main focus would be basic mathematics and physics. Also, given that machine learning is so fashionable these days, I would also be documenting some of my experiments.
